
1: sudo nano /etc/systemd/system/myapp.service

Code:
#################
[Unit]
Description=My App
After=network.target

[Service]
User=ali
WorkingDirectory=/home/ali/app
ExecStart=/home/ali/app/server
Restart=always

[Install]
WantedBy=multi-user.target
#######################################


2:sudo systemctl daemon-reexec
sudo systemctl enable myapp
sudo systemctl start myapp
